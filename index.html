Cinnamon Roll Recipe


Ingredients:


    For the dough:

  • 3½ cups of all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup of granulated sugar
  • 2¼ tsp (1 packet) of active dry yeast
  • ½ tsp of salt
  • ¾ cup of warm milk
  • ¼ cup of unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg

    For the Filling:

  • ⅓ cup of unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup of brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp of ground cinnamon

    For the Glaze:

  • 1 cup of powdered sugar
  • 2 tbsp of milk
  • ½ tsp of vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough: In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, yeast, and salt. Add warm milk, melted butter and egg. Mix until a dough forms.
  2. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 8-10 minutes until it smooths. Place it in a greased bowl, cover it and let it rise for 1 hour or until it doubled in size.
  3. Make the Filling: In a small bowl, mix brown sugar and cinnamon
  4. Roll out the dough into a 12x18-inch rectangle. Spread softened butter over it, then sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar mixture evenly.
  5. Roll the dough tightly into a log, then cut it into 12 even slices.
  6. Place the rolls in a greased baking dish, cover and let it rise for 30 minutes.
  7. Bake:Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Bake for 18-22 minutes or until it's golden brown.
  8. Make the Glaze:Whisk powdered sugar, milk and vanilla until it smooths. Drizzle over warm cinnamon rolls.
  9. Serve warm and enjoy!

Reference: Cinnamon Rolls.
